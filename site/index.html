What it does

Four AI engines, one compliance pipeline

Every gold transaction flows through a single guided process — the dealer sees a simple workflow; the obligation is handled underneath.

KYC & due diligence

Scan an ID, auto-extract the details, and screen against sanctions, PEP and adverse-media lists with a clear risk score.

Transaction reporting

Automatically test the AED 55,000 threshold — including the linked transactions that are easy to miss — and flag what must be reported.

Responsible sourcing

Capture the source of the gold and walk the OECD five-step due-diligence process required under Ministerial Decree 68 of 2024.

goAML filing, drafted

Generate a filing-ready report with a plain-language narrative. You review and submit — nothing is filed automatically.

Why now

The rules changed — the tools didn't

The UAE is the world's second-largest gold trading hub, and its compliance regime was rebuilt in 2024–2025. Ministerial Decree 68 extended responsible-sourcing duties to everyone who trades or handles gold, penalties run to seven figures, and enforcement is active. Yet most dealers still manage all of it on spreadsheets and consultants. AurumTrace is built for exactly that gap.
