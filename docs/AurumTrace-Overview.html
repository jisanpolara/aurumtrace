Product Overview

AML compliance for the UAE gold trade — one guided, inspection-ready workflow.

AurumTrace helps precious-metals dealers meet their DPMS anti-money-laundering obligations end to end: know-your-customer checks, transaction reporting, responsible-sourcing due diligence and goAML filing — turning a fragmented, manual burden into a single calm process.

The challenge

Gold and precious-metals dealers must screen customers, value and record every dealing, judge which transactions are reportable, evidence where their gold came from, and file to the regulator — accurately, in two languages, and ready for inspection at any time. Done by hand across spreadsheets and emails, it is slow, error-prone and hard to defend.

One workflow · six stages

From counter to filing, every step guided and recorded

1

Intake & valuation

Capture the item and customer and value the gold live against the current market price — to AED precision.

2

KYC / CDD screening

Screen the customer against sanctions, PEP and adverse-media lists and score their risk automatically.

3

Threshold check

Detect reportable transactions and aggregate linked dealings so nothing that should be reported is missed.

4

Responsible sourcing

Record OECD five-step supply-chain due diligence and store supporting documents, encrypted.

5

goAML report

Generate a ready-to-file goAML report draft for the compliance officer to review.

6

Audit trail

Every action is captured in a tamper-evident, inspection-ready record of who did what, and when.
